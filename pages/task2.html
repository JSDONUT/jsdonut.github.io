STARBUCKS

Starbucks was opened in March 30, 1971. At first the 3 founders started the journey by purchasing small goods like used roaster from Holland meanwhile learning roasting techniques to create their own blends and flavors. Gradually overtime Starbucks products and brand spread into grocery stores, airlines, hotels and more through alliances with PepsiCo, Kraft Foods, Dreyer's Ice Cream, United Airlines, Starwood Hotels leading to a phenomenal success resulting in presence of Starbucks in 78 countries. Starbucks now features products like non-dairy milk offerings, Ethos water, Instant coffee and coffee capsules and Seasonal cups making Starbucks one of the worlds most renowned café’
