The Structure, Scope, and Size of The Hospitality Industry

✻

Hospitality is the service which is given to customers and guests to entertain and satisfy them which includes many direct and indirect jobs which are in travel and tourism. Direct jobs such as waiting cooking planning events that will entertain the customers can be included. Indirect jobs such as supplying fish which can give people jobs as fishermen. Hospitality services are broadly given at places which tourists visit such as hotels, casinos, resorts, youth hostels, motels, etc...

The scope of the hospitality industry is enormous that common local people in certain countries like the Maldives get benefits from the industry. Many countries' economy is largely benefitted from the profit they get from the hospitality industry. A large majority of the population f a certain country gets jobs that are directly or indirectly connected to the industry.
